Tia Chuy's Story

Tia Chuy began her cookie empire when she bit into a delicious dulce de leche filled sandwich cookie coated in powdered sugar known as an alfajor. She immediately thought, "I can make it better."

After of few months of experimenting with different ingredients and sizes, she perfected the now famous mini-alfajor, the Mexican wedding cookie and the Sugared Horn cookie.

One bite of Tia Chuy's Kookies and everyone was hooked. After taking one bite, family and friends were hooked!
